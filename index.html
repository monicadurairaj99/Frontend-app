OUR STORY

Established in Chennai, India in the year 1964, Chettinad is the pioneer in bringing the food of the famed Chettiars to the people around the world. Over the years, they mastered the art of using spice to give one's taste buds the best food experience. With more than 50 years of experience and 50 locations to date, chettinad is spreading its wings to give customers a homely dining experience. The freshness of its products and the use of secret home ground recipes bring the food lovers in search of those unique dishes that can be found only at chettinad.
